SHORTLY ABOUT US

IODIOMAS is all about the languages and cultures of the world. It is a service where you are free to learn various languages from around the world via your smartphone. Questions are designed to start from a basic level and goes upto advanced level thanks to our pre-established question formats and they are just as easy to answer as well! Questions are usually provided with multiple choices from user has to select the correct translation. IDIOMAS wants to aid all passionate language learners, anytime anywhere.

WHY PEOPLE CHOOSE US

This website would provide users a variety of languages that they could learn. In addition to this, they could easily access various tutorials for any language. The course for every language would start from a basic level and at the end of each tutorial users could take a test to verify what they learnt. Users could post any queries that they have on discussion board. The frequency of tutorials would be customized according to the needs of users. For instance, twice or thrice a week etcetera.
